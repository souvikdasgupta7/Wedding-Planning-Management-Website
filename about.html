Our Story

Who We Are

WeddingPlannerPro is Kolkata's premier wedding planning agency, founded by award-winning event designer Priya Sharma in 2010. What began as a passion project has grown into a team of 25 dedicated professionals who have planned over 500 weddings across India.

We specialize in creating personalized, stress-free wedding experiences that reflect each couple's unique love story. Our team brings together expertise in design, logistics, and cultural traditions to craft celebrations that are as seamless as they are stunning.

From intimate garden ceremonies to grand ballroom receptions, we handle every detail with precision and care, allowing you to focus on what truly matters - celebrating your love.
