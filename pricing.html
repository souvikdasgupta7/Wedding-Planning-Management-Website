Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's the difference between the packages?

The Essential package is perfect for couples who need guidance but want to handle most planning themselves. The Premium package includes full planning services, while the Luxury package offers white-glove service with exclusive vendors and international options.

Can we customize a package?

Yes! We offer add-on services for any package, including extra planning hours, additional consultations, or specific vendor coordination.

When should we book your services?

We recommend booking 12-18 months before your wedding date, especially for our Premium and Luxury packages. Essential packages can be booked 6-9 months in advance.
